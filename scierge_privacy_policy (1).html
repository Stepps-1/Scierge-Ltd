Current Policy

Welcome to SCIERGE. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our Services. By accessing or using SCIERGE, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from third parties. Here is a breakdown of the key categories:

Account Information
Name, email address, phone number, password, date of birth, and profile photo.
Booking Data
Flight searches, reservations, travel documents, seat preferences, and special requirements.
Payment Details
Billing address, card type and last four digits. Full card data is handled by our PCI-DSS certified payment processor.
Location Data
Approximate location derived from IP address; precise GPS if you grant permission in our mobile app.
Usage & Device Data
Pages visited, features used, browser type, OS, device identifiers, and referral URLs.
Communications
Messages sent to our support team, survey responses, and feedback submissions.
Sensitive Data We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passport numbers beyond what is strictly required for travel document verification, which is processed securely and never stored in plain text.
Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, always grounded in a lawful basis:

  • Service Delivery: To process bookings, issue e-tickets, send itinerary confirmations, and provide customer support.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your SCIERGE account, verify your identity, and authenticate logins.
  • Personalization: To tailor flight recommendations, preferred seat suggestions, and destination content to your travel history and preferences.
  • Communication: To send transactional emails (booking confirmations, flight status updates) and, where you have opted in, marketing newsletters and promotional offers.
  • Safety & Fraud Prevention: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions, unauthorized access, and other illegal activity.
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand how our platform is used, diagnose technical issues, and improve features and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and government requests.
We Will Never Sell your personal data to third-party advertisers, use your data to build profiles for resale, or process your data in ways that are incompatible with the purposes stated above without your explicit consent.
Section 03

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party vendors — including airlines, payment processors, cloud infrastructure providers, and analytics platforms — who assist us in operating the Services. These parties are contractually obligated to handle your data only as instructed by SCIERGE and to maintain appropriate security measures.

Airlines & Travel Partners

To complete your booking, we transmit necessary passenger information (name, contact details, passport data where applicable) to the relevant airline or travel partner. Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Legal & Regulatory Obligations

We may disclose your information when required by law, subpoena, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or corporate restructuring, your data may be transferred to the succeeding entity. You will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website prior to the transfer and any changes to this policy.

Section 04

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies (web beacons, pixels, local storage) to enhance your experience on SCIERGE. The categories we deploy are:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for the platform to function — session management, security tokens, load balancing. Cannot be disabled.
  • Performance & Analytics: Help us understand traffic patterns and page performance. We use aggregated, anonymized data.
  • Functional: Remember your language, currency, and seat preferences so you don't have to re-enter them on every visit.
  • Marketing (opt-in only): Used only with your explicit consent to serve relevant travel offers and retargeting ads on third-party platforms.
Cookie Controls You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings panel (accessible from the footer) or through your browser settings. Disabling functional or analytics cookies may impact your experience.
Section 05

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, and reporting obligations. Our standard retention guidelines are:

  • Account Data: Retained for the lifetime of your account and up to 3 years after account deletion, for dispute resolution and fraud prevention.
  • Booking Records: Retained for 7 years to satisfy financial and tax obligations.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 26 months; raw logs deleted within 90 days.
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years after the ticket is resolved.
  • Marketing Opt-in Records: Retained until you withdraw consent, plus 1 year for audit purposes.
Section 06

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security programme includes:

TLS Encryption
All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 or higher.
At-Rest Encryption
Sensitive fields stored with AES-256 encryption at the database level.
Access Controls
Role-based access with multi-factor authentication for all internal systems.
Penetration Testing
Regular third-party security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at admin@scierge.it.com.

Section 07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. We honour these rights for all users globally, not only those in regulated jurisdictions.

Right to Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Right to Rectify
Correct inaccurate or incomplete information in your account.
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
Right to Restrict
Limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
How to Exercise Your Rights Submit a Data Subject Request via our Privacy Portal or email admin@scierge.it.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
Section 08

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at admin@scierge.it.com and we will promptly delete such information.

For bookings involving minors as passengers, we collect only the data required by the airline for check-in and travel, and process it solely for that purpose.

Section 09

International Transfers

SCIERGE operates globally. Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including countries that may not offer the same level of data protection as your home jurisdiction.

When we transfer data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, adequacy decisions, or other legally recognised mechanisms. We require all third-party recipients to maintain appropriate protection for your personal data.

Section 10

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a new effective date.
  • Send a notification email to your registered address at least 14 days before material changes take effect.
  • Display a prominent banner on our platform for 30 days following any significant update.

Your continued use of our Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
