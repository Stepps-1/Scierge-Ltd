Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SCIERGE ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and understand how our platform is used. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to the website owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

🔒 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable core features such as navigation, security, and access to members-only areas. You cannot opt out of these.

⚙️ Functional Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences — such as language, region, and saved packages — to provide a more personalised experience across sessions.

📊 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies (such as those provided by Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site — which pages are most visited, how long sessions last, and where traffic originates. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

🎯 Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing activity to help us deliver relevant advertisements and promotional offers. They may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages, including video players, social media embeds, and analytics providers. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a new cookie is set. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. You may also use the Essential only option in our cookie banner to limit cookies to strictly necessary ones.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
